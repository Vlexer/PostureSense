How does SitApp work?

SitApp uses your device's camera to monitor your posture in real-time. When it detects that you're slouching, it sends a gentle reminder notification to help you maintain better posture. The app runs in the background and doesn't store any video data.

How much does SitApp cost?

SitApp is completely FREE! We believe good posture should be accessible to everyone. There are no hidden fees or premium features - everything is included in the free version.

Is SitApp secure?

Yes, SitApp is designed with privacy in mind. All posture processing happens locally on your device - no video or image data is ever sent to our servers. The app works offline and doesn't require an internet connection.

Does SitApp work if I have a standing desk?

Yes! SitApp can monitor your posture whether you're sitting or standing. It adapts to your desk setup and can help you maintain proper alignment in both positions.
